Empowering Local Communities through Job Creation

Enabling Local Businesses through Green, Clean lighting Solutions

At Ecolight Innovations, our mission extends beyond just providing lighting solutions to rural communities. We are driven by a deep passion for sustainable development, economic empowerment, and improving the overall well-being of individuals in these areas. We are creating opportunities for growth and prosperity.


Our initiative enables local grocery owners to extend their trading hours, significantly boosting their commitment to their businesses and providing community members with access to essential goods during evening hours. we are contributing to the overall development and resilience of these communities.

Meet Moses, a grocery owner from Chilumba community, one of our solar lighting packages üì¶ beneficially. Let's read what he had to say about his experience.

Introduction to EcoLight Innovations

Introduction of EcoLight Innovation Zambia

It is an initiative dedicated to enhancing climate resilience in Zambia by bridging the energy access gap to marginalized communities and improving their overall living standards through empowering them with affordable, reliable, renewable, and eco-friendly solar lighting energy sources, running outreach programs in Schools and Communities on Climate Change Education and Awareness Campaigns, and conducting capacity building workshops.
